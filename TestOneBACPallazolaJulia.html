Each beverage portrayed above represents one standard drink (or one alcoholic drink equivalent), defined in the United States as any beverage containing 0.6 fl. oz. or 14 grams of pure alcohol. The percentage of pure alcohol, expressed here as alcohol by volume (ABV), varies within and across beverage types. Although the standard drink amounts are helpful for the following health guidelines, they may not reflect customary serving sizes.
